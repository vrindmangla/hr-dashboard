# Human Resources Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

The Human Resources Analytics Dashboard is an end-to-end Power BI project designed to analyze workforce trends, employee engagement, compensation, performance, and attrition. The dashboard enables HR professionals and business leaders to monitor key workforce metrics, identify retention risks, evaluate employee satisfaction, and make data-driven decisions to improve organizational performance.

This project transforms raw employee data into actionable insights through interactive dashboards, KPI tracking, and visual analytics.

---

## Business Problem

Employee attrition and workforce engagement directly impact organizational productivity and profitability. HR teams often struggle to identify the factors contributing to employee turnover and dissatisfaction.

This dashboard addresses these challenges by:

- Monitoring workforce performance and engagement.
- Identifying key attrition drivers.
- Evaluating compensation and career growth opportunities.
- Understanding employee demographics and satisfaction.
- Supporting strategic HR decision-making through analytics.

# Dataset Information

The dataset contains employee-level information used for workforce analytics and attrition analysis.

| Column | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Employee ID | Unique identifier assigned to each employee |
| Age | Employee age ranging from 18–60 years |
| Gender | Employee gender |
| Years at Company | Number of years employed at the company |
| Monthly Income | Monthly salary in USD |
| Job Role | Finance, Healthcare, Technology, Education, Media |
| Work-Life Balance | Poor, Fair, Good, Excellent |
| Job Satisfaction | Very Low, Low, Medium, High |
| Performance Rating | Low, Below Average, Average, High |
| Number of Promotions | Total promotions received |
| Distance from Home | Distance between home and workplace |
| Education Level | High School, Associate, Bachelor, Master, PhD |
| Marital Status | Single, Married, Divorced |
| Job Level | Entry, Mid, Senior |
| Company Size | Small, Medium, Large |
| Company Tenure | Total years in industry |
| Remote Work | Yes / No |
| Leadership Opportunities | Yes / No |
| Innovation Opportunities | Yes / No |
| Company Reputation | Very Poor, Poor, Good, Excellent |
| Employee Recognition | Very Low, Low, Medium, High |

# Key Business Insights

### Employee Retention

- Employees receiving promotions demonstrate significantly lower attrition rates.
- Leadership opportunities positively influence retention.

### Work-Life Balance

- Employees reporting poor work-life balance exhibit higher attrition rates.
- Flexible work arrangements contribute to improved retention.

### Career Growth

- Career progression opportunities improve employee engagement.
- Performance ratings correlate positively with promotion frequency.

### Employee Engagement

- High job satisfaction is associated with lower turnover.
- Recognition and innovation opportunities contribute to workforce stability.
